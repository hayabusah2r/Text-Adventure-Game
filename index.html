Lucas is a brave sailor who sails towards uncharted water. One fateful day, a violent storm struck, and his ship was torn apart. He fought to stay afloat, but eventually lost consciousness. When he woke up, he found himself on the shores of a mysterious island, surrounded by a dense, dark forest. Disoriented and alone, brave Lucas ventured into the forest, determined to survive and find a way home. Play as Lucas and help him to escape the forest in the safest way possible.
